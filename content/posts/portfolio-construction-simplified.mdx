Starting an **investment portfolio** can be a daunting prospect. The breadth of assets to choose from is
overwhelmingly massive - from individual **equities** and **bonds** through to **actively managed funds** 
and **ETFs**, it can be hard to know where to start. In this article, we seek to identify a suitable 
selection of assets to invest in - our **universe** of assets - and to **quantitatively** deduce the best 
possible weightings of each of these assets within a portfolio.
