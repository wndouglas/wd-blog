If you're anything like me (i.e. you're an English or Welsh student who started
an undergraduate course anywhere in the UK on or after the 1st September 2012
and you took out a student loan to cover some or all of your fees), you
probably find yourself with a significant student loan to your name.

In this series, we investigate what the best strategy is for repaying your loan.
